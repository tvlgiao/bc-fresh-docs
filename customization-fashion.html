Fashion Style¶

Home Page Section¶

--- Sections Manager¶

You can select the sections displayed on the homepage (maximum 11 sections) in Theme Editor > Homepage > Sections.

List of available sections key:¶

  • fashion-banner
  • fashion-product-tab
  • fashion-banner-product-slider
  • fashion-catalogue
  • fashion-review
  • fashion-blogs
  • fashion-brands
